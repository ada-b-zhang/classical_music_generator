Pasta Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil leaves
- Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
3. Add the cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook until they start to soften.
4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to combine.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Enjoy your pasta!
Simple Cookie Recipe:

- Ingredients:
  - 1 cup of sugar
  - 1 cup of butter
  - 2 cups of flour
  - 1 tsp of baking powder
  - 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  - 1 egg

- Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together sugar and butter.
  3. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Sift flour and baking powder into the mixture.
  5. Mix until well combined.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Let cool and enjoy!
