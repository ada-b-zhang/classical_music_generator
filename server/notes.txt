Pasta Recipe:

Ingredients:
- 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil leaves
- Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sautÃ© until fragrant.
3. Add the cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook until they start to soften.
4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to combine.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Enjoy your pasta!
